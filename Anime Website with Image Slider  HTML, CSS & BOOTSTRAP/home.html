Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is an anime and manga series that follows Tanjiro Kamado, a demon hunter who seeks a cure for his sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon. The story is set in early 20th century Japan, where man-eating demons roam the land each night. The series features stunning animation and brutal combat. It is one of the most popular and profitable franchises in Japan.

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime and manga series that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. He forms a pirate crew called the Straw Hat Pirates and explores the Grand Line, a vast ocean where the legendary treasure One Piece is hidden. Luffy's goal is to find One Piece and become the next King of the Pirates.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a story about a civilization inside three walls, the last place where humans can survive from the threat of the Titans. Titans are giant, mindless creatures that devour humans on sight. The walls are extremely high and protect the city from the Titans' attacks. The story follows the adventures of a group of young soldiers who fight to defend their home and uncover the secrets of the Titans.

My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia is a Japanese manga and anime series about a world where most people have superpowers called Quirks. The series follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without Quirks who dreams of becoming a superhero. He gets a chance to enroll in a prestigious hero academy and learn from the best heroes in the world.
